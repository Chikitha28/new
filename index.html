About Us

Designing dreams and elevating living with JP Housing.

Founded in 2010, JP Group has become one of Bangalore’s leading real estate developers, specializing in residential, commercial, and retail properties. Committed to sustainable growth, the company focuses on creating long-lasting spaces and thriving communities.

With an expanding portfolio across Bangalore's most sought-after neighborhoods, JP Group has successfully delivered over 10 lakh sq. ft. of premium residential spaces, touching the lives of 1,000+ families. Through innovation, attention to detail, and unwavering dedication to customer satisfaction, the company is redefining urban living by creating spaces that offer both style and substance.

With a vision for the future, JP Group continues to shape Bangalore’s skyline, setting new benchmarks in quality, design, and sustainability. Every project reflects the company’s commitment to excellence, blending innovative architecture with eco-friendly practices to create spaces that inspire modern living. From premium residential communities to state-of-the-art commercial hubs, JP Group ensures that every development enhances the urban landscape while prioritizing comfort.
